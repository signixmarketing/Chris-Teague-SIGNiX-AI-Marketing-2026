Stop spending $5,000/month attracting individual notary leads that consume more in staff time than they generate in revenue — redirect that same budget toward FI buyers and ISV developers who generate high-value, recurring-revenue accounts.

## Target Audiences

### Audience 1 — Financial Institution Buyers (Google + LinkedIn)

Decision-makers at banks, credit unions, and lenders evaluating e-sign and authentication for compliance and operational workflows.

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| Titles | VP Operations, IT Director, Director of Lending, Chief Compliance Officer, Director of Digital Banking |
| Institutions | Community banks, credit unions, regional lenders |
| Size | 50–5,000 employees |
| Pain | Fraud risk, examiner readiness, manual signing workflows, authentication gaps |
| SIGNiX message | PKI-based signing + built-in signer authentication = audit-trail compliance without bolting on a separate tool |

### Audience 2 — ISV / Flex API Developers (Google + LinkedIn)

Product managers, engineers, and technical leaders at LOS platforms, fintech companies, and banking software vendors evaluating embedded e-sign APIs.

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| Titles | Product Manager, VP of Product, CTO, VP Engineering, Director of Integrations |
| Companies | LOS platforms, core banking vendors, fintech, regtech, document management |
| Size | 10–500 employees |
| Pain | Building compliant e-sign into their product; regulated industry requirements; white-label need |
| SIGNiX message | Flex API — developer-friendly, white-label, regulated industry compliance built in |

## Google Keyword Strategy ($3,000/month)

Five buckets, each targeting a distinct intent:

| Bucket | Allocation | Top keywords |
|---|---|---|
| Competitor displacement | ~$900 | `docusign alternative for banks`, `docusign alternative credit unions`, `adobe sign alternative compliance` |
| Industry / use-case | ~$1,000 | `electronic signature for banks`, `e-signature for credit unions`, `loan document electronic signature`, `e-signature NCUA compliant` |
| Authentication & security | ~$600 | `e-signature with identity verification`, `KBA electronic signature`, `electronic signature fraud prevention banking` |
| API / developer (Flex API) | ~$500 | `e-signature API financial services`, `white label e-signature API`, `embedded e-signature API` |
| RON institutional only | ~$300 | `remote online notary platform financial institutions` — individual notary terms dropped entirely |

**Excluded:** All individual notary terms ("become a notary," "notary certification," "notary license").

## LinkedIn Approach ($1,500/month)

- **Format:** Sponsored content — single image or document ads; drive to dedicated landing pages
- **Months 1–2 goal:** Validate which audience (FI ops or ISV) engages more → reallocate budget toward top performer
- **Message for FIs:** Fraud and authentication risk angle; mirror Mike's email campaign
- **Message for ISVs:** Flex API capability and white-label ease of integration

---

## How We Know It's Working

**Leading signals (weekly — tell us fast):**
- CTR >3% on priority keyword buckets
- Form fill / demo request rate >2% of paid clicks
- LinkedIn engagement rate >0.5%
- Lead qualification rate >40%

**Lagging proof (monthly/quarterly — prove ROI):**
- Cost per qualified lead by channel vs. notary lead CPL baseline
- Average deal ACV from paid sources vs. notary ACV
- Pipeline sourced ($) attributed to paid channels in HubSpot
- Staff hours per closed deal by source — the hidden metric
