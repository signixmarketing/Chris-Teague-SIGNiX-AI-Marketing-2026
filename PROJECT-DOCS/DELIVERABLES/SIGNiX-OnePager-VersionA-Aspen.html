Signer-Level PKI Signatures
Most platforms seal the document with a server certificate. That proves the export came from their platform. It does not prove who signed. SIGNiX embeds a signer-level certificate directly in the PDF. The document proves who signed, when, and from where. No vendor call required.
Your Data Stays With You
SIGNiX does not store your documents. Many platforms do. Their breach is your exposure. With SIGNiX, there is nothing to steal from our side. One less attack surface for your institution to manage.
ID Verify and Fraud Alert
Authentication is built in, not bolted on. ID Verify confirms a signer's government-issued identity before they sign. Fraud Alert flags suspicious signing behavior in real time. Your audit trail is defensible before a document is ever challenged.
