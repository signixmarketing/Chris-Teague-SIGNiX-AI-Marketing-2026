Most e-sign tools work the same way. You send a document. The platform hosts it. The signer signs on the platform's infrastructure. The completed file lives there until you download it.

That means your vendor's security posture is now part of your risk profile. You have to trust their infrastructure, their access controls, and their breach response. That is on top of your own.

Third-party breach risk

When a major e-sign vendor is breached, every organization that stored documents on their platform is exposed. County governments hold constituent data, vendor contracts, and HR records. A breach does not have to start inside your firewall to become your problem.
