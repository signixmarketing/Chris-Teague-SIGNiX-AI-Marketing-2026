Key Findings
⚠️ Bank of Pontiac is the top priority. It runs ~100 transactions per month at 32% auth rate. That leaves over 1,400 unauthenticated events on the table. SMS alone would add $25.00/month. ID Verify would add $150.00/month. One call with Mike could move this account from 32% to 80%+.
📉 Bank of Old Monroe is losing ground. This is our flagship ID Verify account at $1.50 per event. Usage peaked at 16 events in December. It's down to 1 so far in April. Mike needs to call this account now.
⚠️ 11 accounts are signing documents with no authentication at all. Combined: 544 lifetime transactions and $0 in auth revenue. Turning on SMS for all of them would add $9.11/month. Commercial Bank of TX has 610 more transactions at 0.7% auth — effectively zero too. See the table below for each account's monthly run rate.
🔌 API is at 52% of Q1 2026 volume. MyDoX is at 48%. MyDoX was a bridge when API adoption was slow. The TruStage deal was built on API. API accounts authenticate at 109.9%. MyDoX accounts authenticate at 20.0%. The auth gap lives in MyDoX. See the channel mix chart below.
📈 Volume is growing fast. Q1 2026 is the strongest quarter on record at 2,927 transactions. At this pace, 2026 will reach ~11,708 transactions total. That's 79% above 2025. More volume means more auth opportunity every month.
