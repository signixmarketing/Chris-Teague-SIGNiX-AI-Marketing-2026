What holds up: A PKI-based digital signature. The document is mathematically hashed at the moment of signing. If one character changes after that, the signature breaks. AI cannot forge math.

What SIGNiX does: Every transaction produces a PKI signature plus built-in identity verification. Your audit trail is cryptographic proof, not just a timestamp and an IP address.

Most e-sign tools, including Adobe Sign and DocuSign, create an electronic signature. A person clicks. The system logs an IP address and a timestamp. That is the record.

That record was defensible when fraud required physical effort. It is not defensible when AI can fabricate the evidence that surrounds a click.
