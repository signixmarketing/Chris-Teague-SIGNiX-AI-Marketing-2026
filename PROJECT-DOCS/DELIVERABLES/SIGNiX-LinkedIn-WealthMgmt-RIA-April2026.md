## POST BODY

RIAs get audited. The question is whether your client agreement documentation holds up.

A standard e-sign audit trail shows a click, an IP address, and a timestamp.

That is what you present to a FINRA examiner when a client disputes a transaction.

PKI-based digital signatures work differently. They attach a verified identity and a cryptographic proof to every document at the moment of signing. If the document changes after signing, the signature breaks.

That is not a log. That is proof.

SIGNiX is integrated into some of the largest independent broker-dealer platforms in the country for exactly this reason.

If your client agreement workflow relies on click-to-sign, it is worth a 20-minute conversation before your next audit cycle.
